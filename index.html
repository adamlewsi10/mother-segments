The Origin Story

In French cooking, everything starts with five mother sauces

Béchamel. Velouté. Espagnole. Hollandaise. Tomate. Master these five, and you can create any sauce in the world. They’re the foundation everything else is built on.

We borrowed the idea. In CRM marketing, there are six foundational dimensions that every contact should be tagged against. Get these right, and you can create any campaign, any audience, any personalised message — with precision.

We call them Mother Segments.

What We’re Driving

Commercial outcomes

This isn’t a marketing exercise — it’s a commercial one. Segment-aware, targeted cold outreach that generates sales inquiries and MQLs.

More sales inquiries

Segment-aware, targeted cold outreach that resonates. The right message to the right person prompts replies and meeting requests. The Prospecting Agent compounds this with AI-personalised 1:1 follow-ups to colder prospects.

More MQLs for sales

More contacts will cross the MQL threshold. Segment-aware campaigns drive deeper engagement — opens, clicks, page views, form fills. Each interaction accumulates lead score points. More relevant content = faster MQL conversion.

More Leads for sales

Lead objects auto-create in HubSpot with full segment context. Reps get a prioritised queue of qualified opportunities — persona, function, vertical, customer status — all visible on the Lead record.

The Most Important Segment

Persona definitions

Persona determines the entire tone and depth of content.

Decision Maker

Business transformation, growth, ROI, strategic outcomes. They care what it delivers, not how it works.

User

Day-to-day operator. Practical advice, tips, best practice they can apply immediately.

Super User

Internal champion. Technical depth — automation, integrations, portal management, admin best practice.

The Toolkit

HubSpot tools that power this

Everything runs on tools within HubSpot, supplemented by Apollo.io for enrichment.

Breeze Prospecting Agent
Researches prospects, monitors buying signals, drafts personalised outreach. Uses selling profiles for different personas and markets.
Core sales engine. Configure per segment, pilot on lapsed Decision Makers, scale across all segments.
AI-Powered Email
Generates personalised email copy using CRM data — subject lines, body, follow-ups per contact record.
Pilot: segment-aware campaigns. Scale: 1:1 personalisation driving engagement and MQLs.
Breeze Intelligence
Auto-enriches contacts and companies with 40+ data points. Free for standard fields on Starter+.
Populates raw data for Persona, Function, and Vertical segments.
Data Agent + Smart Properties
AI-researched custom properties. Ask a question, Data Agent fills the answer from CRM + web data.
Auto-fills Persona and Function. Runs via workflow or bulk.
Lead Scoring
Scores contacts on fit (properties) + engagement (behaviour). Predictive model learns from historical data.
Mother Segments feed ‘fit’. MQL threshold triggers Lead creation.
LLMs in Workflows
Custom coded workflow actions using LLMs to classify, route, and enrich contacts. AI-powered branching and segmentation logic at scale.
Auto-classify contacts into Persona and Function. Intelligent routing without manual rules.
Custom Coded Workflows
JavaScript/Python actions inside HubSpot workflows. Call external APIs, run complex logic, transform data.
Apollo enrichment triggers, segment population, multi-step classification pipelines.
Personalisation Agent
Creates tailored website CTAs and content experiences for audience segments.
Website adapts to visitor’s persona and vertical, converting traffic into form fills.
Apollo.io
External enrichment: job titles, departments, seniority levels beyond HubSpot’s dataset.
Supplements Breeze for contacts where HubSpot data is sparse. Bulk via API.
